Conclusion

TaCarla provides a large-scale dataset for end-to-end autonomous driving research with multi-sensor support (camera, BEV, LiDAR-ready). The dataset is designed to support perception, planning, and prediction tasks across diverse Leaderboard 2.0 scenarios.
